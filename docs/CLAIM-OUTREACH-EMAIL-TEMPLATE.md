# Claim outreach – email template for manual sending

When you find a business email (from their website, Google, or directories), use this template for manual outreach. Replace the placeholders.

---

## Subject line

```
Your business "[Business Name]" is listed on FindPro – claim it
```

---

## Plain text (copy-paste)

```
Hi,

[Business Name] has been listed on FindPro.co.za, South Africa's home services directory.

Claim your free listing to add photos, update your details, and reach more customers:

[Claim page URL]

This link takes you to your listing where you can click "Claim this business" and enter your email to receive a one-time claim link. The claim link expires in 7 days.

— FindPro Team
```

**Claim page URL format:** `https://findpro.co.za/business/{slug}`

Example: `https://findpro.co.za/business/robertson-appliance-repairs`

---

```html
<p>Hi,</p>
```

---

## Tips

- **Personalise:** Add their city or category if it helps.
- **Short:** Keep it under 150 words.
- **Clear CTA:** The claim page URL is the main action.
- **Track:** Use the Admin → Listings → Unclaimed outreach section to mark businesses as "contacted" after sending (or use the "Send invite" button to send from the system and auto-mark).
